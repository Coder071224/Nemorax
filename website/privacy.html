Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Nemorax is an AI assistant application that may process account information, conversation history, settings, and feedback data depending on how the deployment is configured.

Information the product may handle

  • Account information such as email address and optional display name.
  • Conversation history for signed-in users where persistent history is enabled.
  • Temporary in-session guest conversations that are not intended to persist across fresh sessions.
  • Feedback reports submitted through the product interface.
  • Operational configuration needed to provide the AI assistant service.

How information is used

Information is used to provide assistant responses, maintain application settings, support account features, retain conversation history where applicable, and improve product quality through feedback and operational troubleshooting.

Guest mode behavior

Guest sessions are designed for temporary use. In-session conversations may remain visible during active use, but they are not intended to persist as account-backed history after a fresh restart.

Signed-in usage

Signed-in users may have account-linked history and settings retained by the backend deployment. Organizations deploying Nemorax should review storage, retention, and access controls before broader public release.

AI providers

Nemorax can be configured to use different AI providers, including local and hosted options. The final privacy posture depends on the provider selected for the deployment environment.

Feedback submissions

If you submit feedback through the app, the message may be stored for product review and operational follow-up. Do not include highly sensitive personal information unless your deployment owner has explicitly approved that workflow.

Deployment responsibility

This privacy policy should be reviewed and adapted by the deployment owner before public production rollout, especially if the service is used outside an internal beta or pilot environment.
