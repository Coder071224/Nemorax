## History

- **Summary:** NEMSU traces its roots to 1982 when Bukidnon State College opened the Bukidnon External Studies Center (BESC) in Tandag City with 153 pioneering students. After several legislative conversions, it became North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU) on July 30, 2021 through Republic Act 11584.
- **Current President:** Dr. Nemesio G. Loayon

### Key Milestones

#### 1982

- **Event:** Bukidnon External Studies Center (BESC) opened in Tandag City; 153 pioneering students; supervised by the late Rufina G. Luna, first College Coordinator.

#### 1992

- **Event:** Surigao del Sur Polytechnic College (SSPC) established via Republic Act No. 7377 signed by President Corazon C. Aquino on April 10, 1992. Merged BESC with schools in Cagwait, Tagbina, Lianga, and San Miguel.

#### 1998

- **Event:** Converted to Surigao del Sur Polytechnic State College (SSPSC) through Republic Act 8628 (April 23, 1998). Surigao del Sur Institute of Technology (SSIT) in Cantilan integrated. Dr. Antonio S. Ang became first President.

#### 2001

- **Event:** Dr. Remegita C. Olvida appointed as second President; renewed in 2003 and 2007.

#### 2010

- **Event:** Converted to Surigao del Sur State University (SDSSU) via Republic Act No. 9998 signed February 22, 2010. Dr. Olvida became first University President.

#### 2014

- **Event:** Dr. Baceledes R. Estal appointed University President by the Board of Regents; served two terms until retirement in 2022.

#### 2018

- **Event:** MOA with University of Southeastern Philippines (USEP) led to turnover of USEP Bislig Campus, becoming SDSSU's 7th campus.

#### 2021

- **Event:** Republic Act 11584 signed on July 30, 2021, renaming SDSSU to North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU).

## Mandate, Vision, and Mission

- **Mandate:** The university shall primarily provide advanced education, higher technological, professional instruction and training in trade, fishery, agriculture, science, education, commerce, engineering, forestry, nautical courses and other related fields. It shall also undertake research and extension services and provide progressive leadership in its areas of specialization (RA 9998, Section 2).
- **Vision:** A Research University advancing technology and innovation for sustainable development.
- **Mission:** We drive sustainable development through quality instruction, innovative research, community collaboration, and technological advancement.

## Goals

- **Instruction:** To develop competitive, resilient and transformative academic community.
- **Research:** To produce high impact research and development for the advancement of knowledge, instructional innovation, institutional development, policy formulation, and commercialization.
- **Extension:** To improve the lives of the rural and urban poor through transfer of technology and knowledge.
- **Production:** To develop and improve resource generation to sustain University operations.
